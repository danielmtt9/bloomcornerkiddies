# Bloom Corner Kiddies — Marketing Template Library (C1.4)

These templates are designed to be copied and pasted directly into WhatsApp. Use the placeholders in `[brackets]`.

---

## 1. Post-Purchase Thank You & Referral Invite
**When to send:** 24 hours after delivery.
**Goal:** Confirm satisfaction and seed the referral program.

> Hi [Parent Name]! Hope [Kid's Name] is loving their new [Product Name] from Bloom Corner! 🌸 
> 
> We wanted to make sure everything fits perfectly. If you have a moment, we'd love to see a photo of them rocking their new outfit!
> 
> **Quick Gift for You:** Since you're now part of the BCK family, I’ve created a unique referral code for you: **[REFERRAL_CODE]**. 
> 
> Share this with a friend—they get **[X]% off** their first order, and once they buy, you get **[X]% off** your next one too! 💛
> 
> — [Your Name], Bloom Corner Kiddies

---

## 2. The "Size-Up" Follow-up
**When to send:** 3–6 months after a purchase (depending on the age group).
**Goal:** Anticipate growth spurts.

> Hey [Parent Name]! It’s been a few months since [Kid's Name] got their [Size] [Product]. They must be growing so fast! 🏃‍♂️💨
> 
> We just got some beautiful new arrivals in [Next Size Up] that I thought would look amazing on them. 
> 
> You can browse the latest collection here: [Your Store Link]
> 
> Shall I set aside a few pieces in [Next Size Up] for you to look at?
> 
> — Bloom Corner Kiddies

---

## 3. New Stock Alert (Broadcast)
**When to send:** Immediately after uploading new products to the admin panel.

> 📦 **JUST IN: The New [Season/Collection Name] is Here!** 📦
> 
> Hi BCK Parents! We’ve just added [Number] new pieces to the shop—including some limited-edition [Category, e.g., Birthday Dresses/School Sets].
> 
> **See what's new:** [Your Store Link]
> 
> These usually go fast, so let me know if you see something you love! We still offer same-day dispatch for orders before 12 PM. 🚚
> 
> — Bloom Corner Kiddies

## 4. Flash Sale / Last Chance
**When to send:** To clear out remaining stock of a specific season.

> ⚡ **FLASH SALE: 24 Hours Only!** ⚡
> 
> We’re making room for our new arrivals! Get **[X]% OFF** everything in the [Category] section.
> 
> 🔗 Shop the sale: [Link to filtered category]
> 
> First come, first served. No holds on sale items! 💛
> 
> — Bloom Corner Kiddies
