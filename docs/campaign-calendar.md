# Bloom Corner Kiddies — Campaign & School Calendar (C1.6)

Nigeria's school calendar and public holidays drive the primary demand peaks for kids' clothing. Use this calendar to plan your stock uploads and broadcast messages.

---

## 📅 The "Peak Demand" Calendar

| Period | Event | Marketing Focus |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| **Late August / Early Sept** | **Back to School** | Durable playwear, "After-school" casuals, and basics. |
| **October 1** | **Independence Day** | "Green & White" themed outfits or "Mini-Patriot" collection. |
| **Late November** | **Black Friday / Pre-Xmas** | Flash sales to clear old stock before the December rush. |
| **December 1 - 24** | **The Christmas Rush** | High-end "Sunday Best", party dresses, and luxury gift sets. |
| **January** | **New Year / Post-Xmas** | "New Year, New Looks" and clearance of winter/heavy items. |
| **February 14** | **Valentine's Day** | "Little Sweetheart" collection (Reds, Pinks, Hearts). |
| **March / April** | **Easter** | Traditional "Easter Sunday" outfits and bright spring colors. |
| **May 27** | **Children's Day 🇳🇬** | **BIGGEST PEAK.** Special "Children's Day" gift sets and celebrations. |
| **June / July** | **End of Session / Grad** | Graduation party wear and "Summer Vacation" sets. |

---

## 🚀 Campaign Planning Checklist

For every peak period (e.g., Children's Day), follow this timeline:

### 3 Weeks Before Peak:
- [ ] **Stock Inventory:** Ensure you have enough sizes in the "hero" items.
- [ ] **Admin Prep:** Upload all new items, photos, and descriptions. Set `is_featured = 1` for the campaign items.
- [ ] **Teaser:** Post 1-2 "Coming Soon" photos on WhatsApp Status.

### 2 Weeks Before Peak:
- [ ] **The "Early Bird" Broadcast:** Send a message to your **Top 10 VIPs** (from the Referrals list) giving them 24-hour early access.
- [ ] **General Launch:** Send the "New Stock Alert" broadcast to your entire contact list.

### 1 Week Before Peak:
- [ ] **The "Last Chance" Reminder:** Send a status update emphasizing that items are selling out (show the "Sold Out" badges in the storefront).
- [ ] **Referral Push:** "Invite a fellow parent to shop the [Event] collection and you both get a gift!"

### Day of Event:
- [ ] **Celebration Message:** A simple "Happy Children's Day" message with no hard sell—just building the brand relationship.

## 📈 Growth Tracking
Once a month, check your **Admin Dashboard**:
1.  **Which category sold best?** (Adjust future stock buys).
2.  **Which gender is most popular?**
3.  **How many new referrals were recorded?**

*Note: As you grow, you can update the `tagline` and `intro_text` in **Admin → Settings** to match the current season (e.g., "Get ready for Children's Day! 🎈").*
